Welcome to Hedane Enterprises

HEDANE ENTERPRISES (T) LIMITED is registered Company in the republic of Tanzania by the register of Companies in 29th JUNE 2017 and awarded certificate of registration no 136145 to conduct the business of public auction, private investigation, repossession of movable securities, realization of Distress for rent and debt collection services.

We have a team of professional and well trained staff who can provide good solutions for all needs as required by the client in Debt collection, Revenue collection, Auctioneers services, Real estate business (selling, buying, leasing etc). Repossession over assets are also one of our specializations.
