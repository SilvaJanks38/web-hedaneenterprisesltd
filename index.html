Welcome to Hedane Enterprises

Hedane Enterprises (T) Limited is a registered and professional company specializing in debt collection, auctioneering, property management, commission agency, and investigative services across Tanzania. Since 2017, we have served banks, financial institutions, corporate bodies, government organizations, and individuals with reliable, lawful, and efficient solutions. Our experienced team is committed to delivering quality, transparency, and prompt service while upholding all legal and regulatory standards
